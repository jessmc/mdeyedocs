Thank you for visiting our website! We have been established in the Jersey Shore area for over 40 years, providing the highest quality of diagnostic services, medical and surgical treatment of eye diseases, and routine eye care. We are a multi-specialty practice with expertise in pediatric ophthalmology, cataract and refractive surgery, retina, glaucoma, and oculoplastic surgery. All of our physicians pride themselves in treating every patient as if they were a family member. From giving a thorough eye examination to making an accurate diagnosis and appropriate treatments, we always put the needs of the patient first.

Our services include: fitting glasses and contact lenses for all ages, LASIK or laser vision correction and laser cataract surgery with premium intraocular lens(IOL) implants for the reduction or elimination of the need for glasses or contact lenses, retina laser and injections for the treatment of diabetic eye disease and macular degeneration, glaucoma laser procedures, and cosmetic procedures such as blepharoplasty(eyelid lift), Botox® and Restylane® injections, and skin resurfacing. Information about these and other services we provide is found on this website under patient education. For further information, please call or email us.
